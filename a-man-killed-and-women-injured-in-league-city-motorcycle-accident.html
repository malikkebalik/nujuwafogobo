A Man Killed and Women Injured in League City Motorcycle Accident

September 13, 2024
blog

League City, TX – A devastating accident has led to the closure of all eastbound lanes on W. FM 646 from W. Walker St. to S. SH-3, as reported by the League City Police Department. The incident, involving a motorcycle, an SUV, and a pick-up truck, unfolded at the intersection of W. FM 646 and S. SH-3, prompting authorities to also close down the intersection.

Tragically, one male lost his life at the scene, while a woman involved in the crash has been rushed to the hospital, according to statements from League City officials. The severity of her injuries remains undisclosed at this time.

The League City Police Department has dispatched a reconstructive accident team to the location to conduct a thorough investigation into the circumstances surrounding the collision. As the community grapples with this unfortunate incident, commuters are advised to seek alternative routes due to the temporary closure of these critical roadways.

Our thoughts are with the victims and their families during this difficult time. Stay tuned for further updates as the investigation unfolds.
