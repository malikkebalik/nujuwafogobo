Who is Danny Duncan?

Danny Duncan is a popular American Youtuber and social media personality.

He is loved by his fans for his sense of humor and lively nature and also has a huge fan following on social media.

Danny Duncan: Professional Life, Career

Danny first worked at a local Walgreens after his high school graduation.

But he got tired of it after a while and decided to try Youtube.

He created his channel on the 7th of March, 2014, and posted his first video in June.
