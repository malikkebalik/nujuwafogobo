Channing Tatum Reveals ‘Ginger’ Pubic Hair

Yes, it’s true — Channing has “ginger” pubic hair. Who knew, right?

The actor revealed on The Ellen Show that he found out he had “some red” when he went to get waxed for Magic Mike XXL.

“After they started they said, ‘Oh, no, you’re a ginger,’ — I have like a little red — and they’re like, ‘It’s always the worse for them.’ And I’m like, then why did you start? Why didn’t you tell me this beforehand?” Channing joked while explaining the process.

Believe it or not, Channing vowed never to wax his private parts again after filming — but he caved for the sequel!

Channing Dances In A Thong In ‘MMXXL’

During his interview, Ellen also spoiled a moment from the film in which Channing has a dance with her pal, DJ tWitch.

“Spoiler alert. We do have a dance together,” Channing shared, to which Ellen asked him, “In thongs?”

“No, there’s just nothing, there’s nothing — very little left to the imagination,” Channing shared.

“That’s why the movie is popular, Channing,” Ellen said, preaching for fans of the film everywhere.
