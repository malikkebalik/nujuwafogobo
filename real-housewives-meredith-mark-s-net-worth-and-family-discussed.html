Real Housewives: Meredith Mark's Net Worth And Family Discussed

August 01, 2024
blog

For you fans of drama and reality TV, the brand new season of The Real Housewives Of Salt Lake City is all set to go on-air on Wednesday, November 11 with six "elite and unconventional women," one of which is the celebrity jewelry designer Meredith Marks.

Introduced as an "ice queen with a knack for partying" in her Bravo bio, Marks comes out as a love-to-hateable character in the first trailer for the much-awaited series. However, for this iteration of the reality show, her marriage will be the point of focus.

The Season Will Revolve Around Meredith Marks' Failing Marriage

Bravo fans held their breath for what seems like forever since the spin-off series was announced at BravoCon 2019. They now have an official trailer and a tonne of backstory drama to catch up on.

As for Marks, she is supposed to be either the villain or a peacemaker this coming season. 

With her party-girl attitude, Nicki Swift reported that she is often hanging out and having fun with fellow castmates Jen Shah and Lisa Barlow. Since the Meredith Marks dimension of the show is set to revolve around her marital problems, the viewers are in for a season for spicy drama and a lot of bickering. 

Meredith Marks Has Been Married For 25 Years

Marks was a Jewish woman who later went on to become a Mormon on her own accord and has managed to stay married for 25 years with husband Seth Stock. The couple even had three kids together: two sons named Reid Marks and Brook Marks; and a daughter named Chloe Marks. But now that the kids are off to college, the extravagant couple is running into marital problems.

As Bravo put it, the ice queen has it all but when push comes to shove and the rumors start to swirl, she is left with but two choices: turn to her girlfriends for support, or keep her troubles to herself and suffer in silence.

Meredith Marks Has A Massive Net Worth Thanks To Her Designer Jewelry Business

Despite her troubled marriage, Marks is not one to back out from an affluent lifestyle as her career yields her handsome profits year after year. Even her fellow castmates Jen and Lisa can't keep themselves from shopping off of the talented designer. When we say "shopping off of," it literally means shopping off as the other two housewives are known to have even bought the rings off her fingers.

Not just her fellow housewives, Marks' designs are celebrity favorites and can be seen donned by the likes of Rhianna, Charlize Theron, Courteney Cox, and many others. Plus, being on one of the hottest franchises on TV doesn't hurt her bottom line either.

According to reports from Cheat Sheet and Yahoo! Finance, the housewives that star on the show make a median average of around a million dollars per season plus bonuses. In addition to that, the Yahoo! site also claims that the fan-favorites are paid dearly just to stay put for future seasons. 

Long story short, the affluent jewelry designer has a massive net worth to back her outgoing lifestyle. In contradiction, her love life at home and drama with the fellow castmembers would probably be what keeps the viewers glued to their seats this season.
