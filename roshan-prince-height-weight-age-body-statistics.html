Roshan Prince is an Indian actor, singer, record producer, musician, songwriter, and music director who has been cast in a number of movies like Lucky Di Unlucky Story, Kabaddi Once Again, Arjan, Aatishbazi ishq, Main Teri Tu Mera, Naughty Jatts, Sirphire, Singh of Festival, Mundeyan Ton Bachke Rahin, Kirpaan – The Sword of Honour, Fer Mamla Gadbad Gadbad, Ishq Brandy, Rang Ratta, and Lagda Ishq Hogaya. In addition to that, Roshan Prince has also worked on various songs including Back To Bhangra, Do Ghutt, The Heart Hacker – Dil Da Chor, Aawaz Punjab Di, Dil Darda, Mr Pendu, Ik Hor Mr Pendu, and Tera Yaar Bolda.
