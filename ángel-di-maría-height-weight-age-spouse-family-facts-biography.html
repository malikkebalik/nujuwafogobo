Ángel Di María is an Argentine professional footballer who is best known for his career with the Ligue 1 club Paris Saint-Germain and the Argentina national team. He plays as a winger or attacking midfielder and is considered to be one of the best wingers in the world. In the past, Ángel has also been a part of a variety of other teams such as Rosario Central, Manchester United, Real Madrid, and Benfica. During the course of his career, he has received numerous honors such as UNFP Ligue 1‘s “Player of the Month”, SJPF‘s “Player of the Month”, and has won several tournaments such as Coupe de France, Coupe de la Ligue, Trophée des Champions, UEFA Super Cup, UEFA Champions League, Supercopa de España, Olympic Gold Medal, FIFA U-20 World Cup, and many more. With Argentina, he finished as the runner-up in the 2014 edition of the FIFA (Fédération Internationale de Football Association) World Cup. He won the 2022 edition of the tournament and scored a goal in the final match against France. This was the 3rd time that Argentina had won the tournament – after 1978 and 1986.
