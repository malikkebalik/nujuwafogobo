On Sunday, June 4, Long Island will be the hip-hop epicenter of the universe.

That night, the annual Hot 97 Summer Jam drops into Elmont, NY’s UBS Arena for one night only bringing some of the genre’s biggest stars along for the occasion.

Just a few of the headliners set to appear include Cardi B, Ice Spice, Coi Leray, Fivio Foreign and French Montana.

They’ll be joined by GloRilla, Lil TJay, Lola Brooke, NLE Choppa and Kenzo B.

Plus, iconic Yonkers natives The Lox aka Sheek Louch, Styles P, and Jadakiss will perform as well celebrating the history of hip-hop.

Thankfully, tickets are still available for the one-night-only event.

In fact, at the time of publication, we found some seats going for a perfect price point: $97 before fees on Vivid Seats.

For fans that want to get closer to the action, lower-level seats can be scooped up for $176 before fees.

$182 before fees is the lowest price available for floor seats.
