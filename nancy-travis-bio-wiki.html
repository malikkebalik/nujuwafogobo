Who is Nancy Travis?

Nancy Travis is an American actress. She is known for her for movies like ‘Three Men and a Baby and its sequel, ‘Three Men and a Little Lady’, ‘Air America’, ‘And Internal Affairs’ among others.

How old is Nancy Travis?

Nancy Travis was born in New York City, New York on September 21, 1961, to Gordon Travis and Theresa. Her father Gordon is a sales executive and her mother Theresa is a social worker.

She spent most of her childhood in Boston, Massachusetts, and Baltimore, Maryland.

Nancy Travis was interested in the theatre world since her early life. Also, she likes zoo sanctuaries.

Nancy graduated from Framingham North High School in 1979. Furthermore, she studied drama at New York University.

Nancy Travis: Career, Profession

Nancy Travis landed her first job after graduating from high school in the play ‘It’s Hard to be a Jew’. Next, she appeared in a stage version of Neil Simon’s ‘Brighton Beach Memoirs’.

Furthermore, she was a founding member of the Off-Broadway theater company Naked Angels. Additionally, she also appeared in their Frank Pugliese play ‘Aven U-Boys’, as well as in King of Connecticut.

Nancy Travis made her Broadway debut in ‘I’m Not Rappaport’.

In addition, she starred in Athol Fugard’s ‘My Children, My Africa’. She played the leading role of psychology professor, Dr. Joyce Reardon, in 2002’s four-hour television adaptation of Stephen King’s Rose Red.

Furthermore, Nancy Travis has appeared in several movies and television series like ‘Work with Me’, ‘Running Mates’, ‘Almost Perfect’, ‘A Walk in My Shoes, ‘How I Met Your Mother’, ‘Hart of Dixie, ‘Last Man Standing’, ‘Fallen Angels’, ‘Tales from the Darkside’, ‘3 Men and a Baby’ and other.

Nancy Travis garnered the Prism Award in 2008 for ‘The Party Never Stops Diary of a Binge Drinker’ in the category of Performance in a TV Movie or Miniseries.

In 2021 Nancy Travis played the role of Halen Cooper in the famous American adult animated zombie horror film, Night of the Animated dead.
