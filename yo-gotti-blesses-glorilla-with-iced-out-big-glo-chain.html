GloRilla has added a new jewelery piece to her collection thanks to her CMG Records boss Yo Gotti.

The Memphis rapper has been on a hot streak ever since signing to CMG earlier this year. As a token of his appreciation for her grind, Gotti gifted GloRilla with an icy “Big Glo” chain over the weekend.

GloRilla has stacked up quite the list of wins as of late, going from a relatively unknown artist to receiving her first Grammy nomination in the Best Rap Performance category for “F.N.F” last week.

“The same song they was clowning me & my friends about saying we was Ugly & Dusty just got nominated for a Grammy,” she wrote on Twitter. “WHEN GOD IS ON YOUR SIDE YOU CANNOT FAIL.”

Glo’s Cardi B-assisted “Tomorrow 2” has also been a Hot 100 staple where it currently sits at No. 29 this week.

The Memphis native is also gearing up to embark on her first headlining tour, which kicks off on January 27 in Charlotte, North Carolina. The Anyways, Life’s Great… Tour will make stops in Houston, Chicago, Atlanta, Las Vegas, New York City and more before closing things out in Washington D.C. on February 25.

The trek comes in support of her EP of the same name, which sits behind rap royalty on the Billboard 200 while debuting at No. 11 and just one slot behind Nas’ King’s Disease III album.

Nas even shouted Big Glo out on the project’s closing bonus track “Til My Last Breath,” where he raps: “I’m applying pressure, I see why she prеssed (Why she pressed)/ When she with me she GloRilla, FNF (FNF)/N-A-S, I’m steppin’ ’til my last breath (My last breath, yeah).”
