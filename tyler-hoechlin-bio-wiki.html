Who is Tyler Hoechlin?

Tyler Hoechlin is a young and talented American actor

He is best known for his role as Michael Sullivan Jr. in the 2002 film ‘Road to Perdition’ and also for the role of Martin Brewer in ‘7th Heaven‘.

This American actor has starred as Derek Hale on MTV’s ‘Teen Wolf’ and ‘Everybody wants some!!’

He was born to Lori and Don Hoechlin during the year 1987 in Corona, California, United States of America.

He was brought up by his parents along with his brothers Tanner and Travis and a sister Carrie.

His father was of Swiss-German descent and his mother was of English, German, Norwegian, Scottish, and Irish descent.

Tyler Hoechlin: Career, Salary, and Net Worth

As a struggling actor, Tyler Hoechlin gave an audition for the role of Max in ‘Charlie’s Angels: Full Throttle’ but, lost with Shia LaBeouf which was very disappointing for him.
