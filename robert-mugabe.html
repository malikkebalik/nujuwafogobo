Biography

Robert Mugabe was born in Kutama, Zimbabwe on February 21, 1924. Zimbabwean leader who served as Prime Minister from 1980 to 1987 and as President from 1987 until 2017. He also chaired the Zimbabwe African National Union from 1975 until 1980. He met future leader Kenneth Kaunda while studying in South Africa during the early 1950’s. After graduation, he was employed as a lecturer at the Chalimbana Teacher Training College.
