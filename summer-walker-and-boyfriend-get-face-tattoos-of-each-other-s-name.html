Summer Walker and Boyfriend Get Face Tattoos of Each Others Name

September 18, 2024
blog

American R&B singer Summer Walker has made a few revelations recently. A few hours prior to this writing, Summer took to her Instagram account and posted a video that has since gone viral. 

In the said video, Walker confirmed her boyfriend, who seemingly goes by the name of Lary, with their freshly crafted face tattoos.

As far as their inked design is concerned, the couple decided to get each other's names written next to their eyebrows. 

The video clip, accompanied by a background song, is a compilation of three images of the newly-announced couple showcasing their relationship. 

Summer Walker Kept Her New Boyfriend's Identity A Secret

Before making her relationship with Lary official, Walker had kept his identity under the wraps for quite some time.

However, eagle-eyed fans watched closely when the couple gave out hints of their relationship earlier in September. 

When the singer tried her best to maintain the mystery, an unintentional clue slipped out from their hands in the form of social media posts.

Firstly, Walker posted a short clip of a hallway filled with rose petals and candles on her Instagram account. Interestingly, her then-unknown boyfriend posted a picture of the same hallway around the same time. 

Additionally, in a report by the Jasmine Brand, the singer posted a series of pictures on her now-deleted Instagram account on September 30, 2021. She was seen spending quality time with her current boyfriend.

However, the singer prioritized her boyfriend's identity as his face was hidden with a black facial covering as seen in the shared pictures.

And in the caption of the post, Walker wrote how she "can't wait to" become a mother for the "second time" and experience "real pregnancy," with her new boyfriend, apparently confirming her relationship.    

Walker is London on da Track's Baby Mama

Before Walker was dating Lary, her love life with music producer London on da Track was known publicly.

While Walker had an on-and-off relationship with her ex-boyfriend, she became his baby mama after the birth of their child, a daughter, earlier in 2021.

According to Capital Xtra, their dating history dates back to 2019 while the singer was busy working on her debut album.

But unfortunately, the former couple decided to go their separate ways after things did not work out between them.

After her relationship with the music producer was over, Walker vented her frustration as she took to her social media and made a series of allegations against her ex-boyfriend, as per a report published in the Jasmine Brand.

In her accusations, Walker claimed London on da Track to be a "ghetto baby daddy from hell" who was asking "everyone around me" to "get that [expletive] away from her."

In the same light, Walker stated that if anything wrong was to happen to her, "it was him." She further asked her ex to stop sending death threats to her current boyfriend.

The record producer responded to the allegations made against him by his former girlfriend and claimed them to be untrue.
