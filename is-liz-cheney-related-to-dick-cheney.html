Who is Liz Cheney? How is She related to Dick Cheney?

From 2016 until her defeat on May 12th, 2021, Elizabeth Lynne Cheney, 54, was a Republican member of the United States House of Representatives for Wyoming.

In Casper, Wyoming, Cheney grew up and went to school while her father was working in the White House. The University of Chicago Law School awarded her a Juris Doctorate in 1996 after she earned a Bachelor of Arts degree from Colorado College in 1993.
