Early Life

During his high school years, Astley honed his musical skills by forming local bands and playing drums on the Northern Club circuit.

Astley’s big break came when he caught the attention of record producer Pete Waterman, who recognized his potential and offered him a recording contract. Astley’s debut single, “When You Gunna,” marked the beginning of his journey as a solo artist, laying the groundwork for his meteoric rise to fame.

In 1987, Astley unleashed his signature hit, “Never Gonna Give You Up,” a dance-pop anthem that captured the hearts of millions worldwide.
