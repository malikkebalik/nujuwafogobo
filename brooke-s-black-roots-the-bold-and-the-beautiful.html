BROOKE'S BLACK ROOTS - The Bold and the Beautiful

September 13, 2024

keltoni January 30, 2007, 10:33pm 1

Why is it that Brooke has all the time & money but can’t find time to dye her hair.
Today when she was talking to Nick they showed her hair and her black roots were terrible. Can’t believe after all these years I now know she is truely a brunette…

Being a hairstylist, I notice things like that also

I never understand why someone who dyes their hair would even allow one scrap of their regular hair colour to show through.

It’s a pet peeve of mine as well. I want to take them and colour their hair!

In defense of hair “colorers” everywhere…it only takes about 3 to 4 weeks for your “growth” to show. I know because I’m a near black haired person who colors gray! Every 4 weeks! And near the end, I can’t wait to get back into that stylists chair because you can about a 1/4 inch of gray strands at the part!

So, even though I see how ugly it looks when a brunette who has gone completely blonde (the black roots), I have a little sympathy. She just needs to make time to get to her colorist every 4 weeks. If she has a strip down the top of her skull, she’s waited too long!

Color me wrong (no pun intended)…but I think Brooke would actually look better as a brunette. :]
